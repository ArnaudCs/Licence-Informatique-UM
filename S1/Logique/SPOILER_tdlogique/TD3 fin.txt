1. ∀x.P(x) ⇒ ∃y.P(y) ∨ Q(y)

                 
----------------------------------------------------------------- ax   
			P(x) ⊢ P(x) , Q(x)
----------------------------------------------------------------- v right 
			P(x) ⊢ P(x) ∨ Q(x)
---------------------------------------------------------------- ∃ right
		   P(x) ⊢ ∃y.(P(y) ∨ Q(y))
-------------------------------------------------------------- ⇒ right  
           ⊢ P(x) ⇒ ∃y.P(y) ∨ Q(y)
------------------------------------------------------------- ∀ right
           ⊢ ∀x.(P(x) ⇒ ∃y.P(y) ∨ Q(y))

           
           
           
2. (∃x.P(x) ∨ Q(x)) ⇒ (∃x.P(x)) ∨ (∃x.Q(x))

														
-------------------------------------- ax                       --------------------------------------- ax
       P(x) ⊢  P(x) ,  (∃x.Q(x))                                         Q(x)  ⊢  (∃x.P(x)) ,  Q(x)
-------------------------------------- ∃ right                 --------------------------------------- ∃ right  
    P(x) ⊢  (∃x.P(x)) ,  (∃x.Q(x))                                 Q(x)  ⊢  (∃x.P(x)) ,  (∃x.Q(x))
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- v left
						             P(x) ∨ Q(x)  ⊢  (∃x.P(x)) ,  (∃x.Q(x))
	                        ------------------------------------------------------------ ∃ left
								    (∃x.P(x) ∨ Q(x))  ⊢  (∃x.P(x)) ,  (∃x.Q(x))
	                       --------------------------------------------------------------- v right 
									(∃x.P(x) ∨ Q(x))  ⊢  (∃x.P(x)) ∨ (∃x.Q(x))
	                         --------------------------------------------------------------- ⇒right
									⊢ (∃x.P(x) ∨ Q(x)) ⇒ (∃x.P(x)) ∨ (∃x.Q(x))





3. (∀x.P(x)) ∧ (∀x.Q(x)) ⇒ ∀x.P(x) ∧ Q(x)


     ----------------------- ax                                   --------------------- ax
       P(x), Q(x) ⊢ P(x)                                      P(x), Q(x) ⊢ Q(x) 
     --------------------------------------------------------------------- ∧ right
                                P(x) , Q(x) ⊢ P(x) ∧ Q(x)
                       ------------------------------------------------------ ∀ left
                               P(x) , ∀x.Q(x) ⊢ P(x) ∧ Q(x)
                        ---------------------------------------------------- ∀ left
                             ∀x.P(x) , ∀x.Q(x) ⊢ P(x) ∧ Q(x)
                     ------------------------------------------------------- ∧ left
                        (∀x.P(x)) ∧ (∀x.Q(x)) ⊢ P(x) ∧ Q(x)
                 --------------------------------------------------------- ∀ right
                        (∀x.P(x)) ∧ (∀x.Q(x)) ⊢ ∀x.P(x) ∧ Q(x)
              ----------------------------------------------------------- ⇒ right
                   ⊢ (∀x.P(x)) ∧ (∀x.Q(x)) ⇒ ∀x. P(x) ∧ Q(x)




4. (∀x.P(x) ∧ Q(x)) ⇒ (∀x.P(x)) ∧ (∀x.Q(x))


------------------------------ ax                                           ------------------------- ax       
            P(x),Q(x) ⊢ P(x)                                                       P(x),Q(x) ⊢ Q(x)
 -------------------------- ∧ left                                        -------------------------- ∧ left
       P(x)∧ Q(x) ⊢ P(x)                                                       P(x)∧ Q(x) ⊢ Q(x)
    -------------------------- ∀ left                                   --------------------------- ∀ left
		∀xP(x)∧ Q(x) ⊢ P(x)                                                 ∀x.P(x)∧ Q(x) ⊢ Q(x)
----------------------------------------- ∀ right              ------------------------------- ∀ right
		∀x.P(x)∧ Q(x) ⊢ ∀x.P(x)                                            ∀x.P(x)∧ Q(x) ⊢ ∀x.Q(x)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ ∧ right
									∀x.(P(x) ∧ Q(x)) ⊢ (∀x.P(x)) ∧ (∀x.Q(x))
								------------------------------------------------- ⇒ right
								⊢(∀x.P(x) ∧ Q(x)) ⇒ (∀x.P(x)) ∧ (∀x.Q(x))



Exemple de renommage :
    
----------------------------- ax
P(x), P(y)  ⊢ P(y)
----------------------------------- ¬ right                       
P(x) ⊢ P(y), ¬P(y)
---------------------------------- ∃ right
P(x) ⊢ P(y), ∃x.¬P(x)
----------------------------------- ∀ right
P(x)  ⊢ ∀x.P(x), ∃x.¬P(x)
